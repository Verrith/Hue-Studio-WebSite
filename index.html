ABOUT
US

At HUE Studio, creating experiences that push the limits of storytelling and explore unique visual styles is what we do. We work with talented developers and artists to bring new visions to life in the world of video games and animation.

We believe the interactive nature of video games can be a powerful tool for the research field and offer services to help the development of such projects.

As a women-led studio, we are passionate about creating an inclusive space where the quieter voices can resonate and be amplified.
